Multimodal AI systems process more than one kind of input, such as text, images, audio, and video. A multimodal assistant might read an image, answer a question about it, and then generate a text summary. These systems are useful for medical imaging support, document understanding, visual question answering, robotics, and accessibility tools.

One challenge in multimodal modeling is aligning information across modalities. The system has to connect language tokens with image regions or audio patterns in a meaningful shared representation. Training data quality matters because poor alignment data can reduce performance even when the model is large. In production systems, multimodal pipelines also need latency control, content filtering, and clear fallback behavior when one modality is missing or noisy.
